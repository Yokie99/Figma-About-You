Accomplishments

  • Hall of Famer
    • With my mighty water cannons and unwavering spirit, I've battled alongside my trainer, conquering countless foes and proving myself time and again. The honor of being enshrined in the Pokémon Hall of Fame is a tribute not only to my power but also to the unbreakable bond I share with my trainer and team. Together, we've weathered every challenge and obstacle, demonstrating that true greatness emerges when a Trainer and their Pokémon work in perfect harmony.

  • The Great Cineabar Fire
    • When I was a young squritle I was only a firefighter in training. I crossed the ocean to Cinebar Island to enroll in their fire academy. After a month into the program is when the Great Cinebar Fire started. Thanks to the squritle squad junior cadets, we managed to douse the spread of the fire to the south side of the island. This has been the largest fire I have fought and I am proud of the buildings and lives that I saved that day.
